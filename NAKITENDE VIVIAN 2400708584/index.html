Quality education is one of the most powerful tools for transforming lives and communities. SDG 4 seeks to ensure that everyone—regardless of gender, background, or location—has access to inclusive and equitable quality education and lifelong learning opportunities.

Targets of quality education

Free, equitable primary and secondary education.
Equal access to technical and higher education.
Eliminate gender disparities in education.
Increase literacy and numeracy.
Build safe and inclusive learning environments.
